Depth Layer — 5 Quick Captures

Beneath the surface.

These five micro-captures cover the remaining COMPASS domains. They are short and direct — 1–3 sentences each is enough. They fill in what the six main questions don't fully surface.

Behavior — Loops

What pattern do you keep repeating that you already know isn't working?

Be specific. Name the loop, not the category.

Emotion — Baseline

What emotional state are you operating from most often right now?

Not a story — just the state. Frustrated? Flat? Driven? Scattered?

Strategy — Constraint

What is the single biggest constraint on your ability to move forward right now?

Time, money, clarity, people, systems, confidence — name the real one.

Renewal — Restore

What reliably restores you when you are depleted?

Not what should — what actually does.

Renewal — Drain

What is currently draining the most energy without producing proportional return?

An activity, relationship, role, or commitment. Name it honestly.
