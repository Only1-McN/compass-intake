Depth Layer — Optional but Valuable

One layer deeper.

These five questions take the surface answers and find the structural signal underneath. Skip any that don't land — the ones that do are usually the most important.

Behavior
What behavior do you keep repeating — even when you know it's not working?
The loop that restarts itself. Not a failure — a pattern worth naming.
Emotion
What emotional state do you operate from most consistently — even when you don't want to?
Your default frequency. This shapes every decision whether you name it or not.
Strategy
What is the one constraint — real or assumed — that is most limiting your direction right now?
Resources, permission, time, belief — what is the actual bottleneck?
Renewal
What restores you — genuinely, not aspirationally?
Not what you think should restore you. What actually does.
Energy Drain
What is consuming energy that should be going somewhere else right now?
A commitment, relationship, activity, or thought pattern that you are still carrying.
